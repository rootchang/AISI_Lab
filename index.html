Principal Investigator

Dr. Tiangen Chang

PhD in Computational Biology at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. Postdoctoral Fellow at U.S. National Cancer Institute. His research program lies at the intersection of clinically driven AI and mechanistic systems biology, with a focus on decoding the complex, multi-scale interplay between the tumor microenvironment and the systemic immune system. He has published as first and/or corresponding author in premier journals, including Nature Cancer, Science Immunology, Cancer Discovery, and Annals of Oncology.
